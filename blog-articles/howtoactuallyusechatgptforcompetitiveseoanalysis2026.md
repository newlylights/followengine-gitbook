## FAQ
### Can ChatGPT replace SEO tools?
No. ChatGPT lacks access to real-time data — it can't tell you who's running ads, who changed pricing, or what keywords are actually moving.
### How accurate is ChatGPT for keyword research?
Good for generating ideas and understanding intent. Terrible for search volume, competition level, or actual ranking difficulty. Always validate with real data.
### What's the fastest way to improve SEO analysis accuracy?
Combine AI strategy generation (ChatGPT) with real-time market validation (FollowEngine).
### Why does real-time competitive intelligence matter for SEO?
Because most competitive moves — ad campaigns, pricing changes, product launches — happen long before they show up in SEO rankings.
### How do you know if your SEO strategy is based on reality?
If your keyword priorities don't change when you check what competitors are actually investing in, you're operating on assumptions.
