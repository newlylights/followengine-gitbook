## Keep It Fresh

Battle cards decay quickly. A pricing change, feature launch, or strategy shift can make yesterday's battle card obsolete. Platforms like [FollowEngine](https://followengine.com) monitor competitors in real time and surface the signals that should trigger a battle card update.
