# CI KPIs & Measurement

How to measure, prove, and optimize the return on your competitive intelligence program.

## The Measurement Challenge

[Gartner](https://www.gartner.com/en/marketing/insights/articles/why-your-competitive-intelligence-program-isnt-working) reports that only **29%** of CI programs can quantitatively demonstrate ROI. Yet organizations that measure CI impact are **4x more likely** to secure increased investment.

## The CI KPI Pyramid

```
         ┌──────────────────────┐
         │   REVENUE IMPACT      │
         │   Win rate, deals,    │
         │   retention, pricing  │
         ├──────────────────────┤
         │   ACTIVATION           │
         │   Adoption, usage,    │
         │   response rate       │
         ├──────────────────────┤
         │   OUTPUT               │
         │   Volume, coverage,   │
         │   speed, accuracy     │
         └──────────────────────┘
```

## Tier 1: Output Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Intelligence Volume | # of intelligence items/month | >50 |
| Competitor Coverage | % of competitors actively monitored | 100% |
| Speed-to-Alert | Time from event → published intel | <4 hours |
| Accuracy Rate | % of intel verified as accurate | >95% |

## Tier 2: Activation Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Intel Adoption Rate | % of target audience consuming CI | >60% |
| Battle Card Usage | % of competitive deals using battle cards | >80% |
| Alert Response Rate | % of alerts actioned within SLA | >70% |
| Intel NPS | Internal satisfaction score | >30 |

## Tier 3: Revenue Impact Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Win Rate Lift | Improvement in competitive win rate | >+5% |
| Deal Influence | % of won deals citing CI | >30% |
| Revenue Protected | $ at-risk revenue saved via CI | Track quarterly |
| Pricing Optimization | Revenue impact of CI-informed pricing | Track annually |

## CI ROI Calculation

```
CI ROI = (Revenue Impact − CI Program Cost) / CI Program Cost × 100%

Revenue Impact = (Deal Influence Revenue × 0.15)
                + Churn Prevention Revenue
                + Pricing Optimization Uplift
```

### Example

| Component | Annual Value |
|-----------|-------------|
| CI Platform + Analyst + Data | $65,000 cost |
| Deal Influence ($5M × 15%) | $750,000 |
| Churn Prevention | $200,000 |
| Pricing Optimization | $150,000 |
| **Total Revenue Impact** | **$1,100,000** |
| **CI ROI** | **1,592%** |
