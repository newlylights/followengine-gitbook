# FollowEngine: Competitor Growth Intelligence

> Follow competitors. Copy what works.

## Overview

[FollowEngine](https://followengine.com) is a competitor growth intelligence platform that captures competitor digital signals and turns them into actionable marketing and product moves.

## What FollowEngine Monitors

- **Ads** — New creatives, copy, landing pages, and offer angles across Google, Meta, TikTok
- **Search** — Keyword shifts, visibility movements, and intent changes
- **Content** — YouTube, TikTok, social cadence, and breakout themes
- **Competitors** — Positioning shifts, launches, campaigns, and channel changes
- **Channels** — Creator and content growth tracking independent of full brands
