# What is Competitive Intelligence?

## Definition

**Competitive Intelligence (CI)** is the systematic collection, analysis, and application of information about competitors, market trends, and industry dynamics to inform strategic decision-making. It transforms raw competitor data into actionable business insights.

## Why CI Matters

Organizations with mature CI programs are **3x more likely** to outperform revenue targets, according to [Gartner](https://www.gartner.com/en/marketing/insights/daily-insights/why-cmos-must-lead-competitive-intelligence). CI is not corporate espionage — it's the ethical and legal gathering of publicly available information to make smarter business decisions.

## The CI Lifecycle

```
Plan → Collect → Analyze → Disseminate → Act → Monitor → (repeat)
```

### 1. Plan
Define what you need to know. Which competitors? What questions are you trying to answer? What decisions will this intelligence inform?

### 2. Collect
Gather data from:
- **Public sources:** Websites, press releases, earnings calls, job postings
- **Digital signals:** Ads, SEO changes, content strategy, social media
- **Product intelligence:** Feature launches, pricing changes, review sentiment
- **Market data:** Industry reports, patent filings, regulatory filings

### 3. Analyze
Transform raw data into intelligence using frameworks like Porter's Five Forces, SWOT analysis, and win/loss pattern recognition.

### 4. Disseminate
Deliver intelligence to stakeholders in formats they'll use:
- Sales teams → Battle cards
- Product teams → Feature gap analysis
- Executives → Strategic briefs
- Marketing → Competitive positioning maps

### 5. Act
Close the loop. Intelligence that doesn't drive decisions is just data.

### 6. Monitor
CI is continuous, not one-off. Competitors move daily — your intelligence should too.

## Types of Competitive Intelligence

| Type | Focus | Example Questions |
|------|-------|-------------------|
| **Strategic CI** | Long-term market position | Where is the market heading? |
| **Tactical CI** | Short-term competitive moves | What are they launching next? |
| **Technical CI** | Product and technology | What's in their tech stack? |
| **Market CI** | Industry trends and forces | What's shifting in our TAM? |

## The Modern CI Stack

Traditional CI relied on manual research and quarterly reports. Modern CI uses AI-powered platforms like [FollowEngine](https://followengine.com) to automate the collection and initial analysis phases — freeing CI professionals to focus on strategic insight and stakeholder activation.
