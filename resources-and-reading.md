# Resources & Further Reading

Curated resources for deepening your competitive intelligence practice.

## Books

- **Competitive Strategy** — Michael E. Porter (1980)
  *The foundational text on competitive analysis. Still the starting point after 40+ years.*

- **Playing to Win** — A.G. Lafley & Roger L. Martin
  *Strategy framework for building and sustaining competitive advantage.*

- **The Art of War** — Sun Tzu
  *Timeless principles applied to modern business competition.*

- **Blue Ocean Strategy** — W. Chan Kim & Renée Mauborgne
  *Creating uncontested market space rather than fighting in red oceans.*

- **Competitive Intelligence Advantage** — Seena Sharp
  *Practical CI implementation for business decisions, not just theory.*

- **Good Strategy Bad Strategy** — Richard Rumelt
  *How to distinguish real strategy (with competitive insight) from wishful thinking.*
