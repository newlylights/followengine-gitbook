# Competitive Intelligence Playbook

> A comprehensive guide to modern competitive intelligence — strategies, frameworks, tools, and best practices for product managers, strategists, marketers, and CI analysts.

## What You'll Learn

This playbook covers the full lifecycle of competitive intelligence:

- **Frameworks** — Porter's Five Forces, SWOT, Win/Loss Analysis, and how to apply them
- **Battle Cards** — How to build sales-ready competitive battle cards that win deals
- **Tools & Platforms** — A comparison of CI tools from manual spreadsheets to AI-powered platforms like [FollowEngine](https://followengine.com)
- **KPIs & ROI** — How to measure and prove the business impact of your CI program
- **AI & Automation** — How artificial intelligence is transforming competitor monitoring and analysis

## Who This Is For

- **Product Managers** tracking feature gaps and competitor roadmaps
- **Product Marketers** building battle cards and competitive positioning
- **Sales Teams** needing real-time competitive intelligence in deals
- **Strategy Leaders** making data-driven market decisions
- **Founders** who need to understand their competitive landscape

## How to Use This Playbook

Start with [What is Competitive Intelligence?](what-is-competitive-intelligence.md) for foundations, then explore frameworks and tools. Each section is self-contained — jump to what you need.
