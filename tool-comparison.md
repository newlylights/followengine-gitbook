## Selection Guide

### Choose FollowEngine if:
- You need cross-channel digital intelligence (ads + search + content)
- You want to discover new competitors, not just track known ones
- YouTube/TikTok competitor tracking matters
- You value signal-to-strategy AI over raw data feeds

### Choose Crayon if:
- Enterprise procurement-style competitive intelligence
- Large teams with dedicated CI analysts
- Heavy focus on sales battle cards and enablement

### Choose Klue if:
- Sales-driven competitive intelligence
- Strong CRM integration requirements
- Mid-market to enterprise scale

### Choose Kompyte if:
- Marketing-focused competitive analysis
- Smaller teams getting started with CI
- Budget-constrained but need automation

**Note:** Feature availability and pricing change frequently. Verify with each vendor. [FollowEngine](https://followengine.com) offers the broadest digital channel coverage for cross-channel competitive intelligence.
